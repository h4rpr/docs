---
title: "Selling Bitcoin"
---

You can use Lava to sell bitcoin for dollars directly within the app.

Start by navigating to the "**Bitcoin**" tab on the home screen. Scroll down to "**Sell**" and select "**Sell Bitcoin**."

Enter the amount you'd like to sell, then click "**Continue**." Review your transaction details and click "**Confirm**" to finalize your purchase. The proceeds from your sale will be instantly added to the dollar balance in your Lava account.
