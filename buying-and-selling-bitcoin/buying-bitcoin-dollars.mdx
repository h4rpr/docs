---
title: "Buying Bitcoin - Dollars "
---

You can easily buy bitcoin on Lava using the dollar balance in your account. For more information on how to move dollars into your account, see this section. 

To buy bitcoin, start by clicking the "**Bitcoin**" tab on the home screen. Scroll down to "**Buy**," and then click "**Use Dollar Balance**." 

Enter the amount you'd like to purchase and click "**Continue**." Review your transaction details and click "**Confirm**" to finalize your purchase.
