---
title: "Depositing Dollars - Bank Account "
---

On the home screen, click on "**Add Dollars**" below the "**Dollars**" tab.

Select "**Choose Adding Option**" and then select "**Transfer from Bank**."

From there, you'll see the information for your virtual dollar bank account. You can send money from your local bank account to this virtual account, and those dollars will be deposited into your Lava account.
