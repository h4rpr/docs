---
title: "Direct Deposit - Bitcoin"
description: "Description of your new file."
---

You can use Lava's bitcoin direct deposit feature to get paid in bitcoin directly to Lava from your paycheck.

On the home screen, click on the "Bitcoin" tab at the top.

Scroll down to "Buy" and select "Direct Deposit."

From there, you'll see the information for your virtual bitcoin bank account. Direct your payroll to that virtual account, and your full paycheck will be instantly converted to BTC and added to your bitcoin balance on Lava.
