---
title: "Depositing Dollars - Stablecoins "
---

On the home screen, click on "**Add Dollars**" below the "**Dollars**" tab.

Select "**Choose Adding Option**" and then select "**Receive Dollars**."

This will generate a deposit address. You can send USDC on Solana to this address, and it will be added to your dollar balance in Lava.
