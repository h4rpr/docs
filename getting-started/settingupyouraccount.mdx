---
title: "Setting Up Your Account"
---

To get started with Lava, [download the Lava app](https://www.lava.xyz/) on iOS or Android. 

Then, open the app, tap "Create Lava Account," and follow these steps: 

1. Enter your email address
2. Create and confirm your password (make sure you keep this securely backed up somewhere safe)
3. Enable notifications so that we can easily send you important updates related to your account

Once you complete these steps, you'll see the account home page where you can deposit funds.
