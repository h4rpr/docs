---
title: "Sending Bitcoin"
---

Click the ↑↓ middle tab in the bottom navigation bar of the app.

Click the drop down menu to select "**Bitcoin**" as your payment currency.

Enter the amount you'd like to send. Click the "**Send**" button. Scan a QR code or paste in an address.

Confirm your transaction details, then press "**Send**" to initiate the transaction.

Note that bitcoin transactions can take 10-60 minutes to confirm. You can navigate to your transaction history in the top right corner of the home screen to see the status of your transaction.
