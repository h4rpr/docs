---
title: "Sending Dollars "
---

Click the ↑↓ middle tab in the bottom navigation bar of the app.

Click the drop down menu to select "**Dollars**" as your payment currency.

Enter the amount you'd like to send, then click the "**Send**" button. 

Scan a QR code or paste in an address.

Confirm your transaction details, then press "**Send**" to initiate the transaction.

Dollar transacitons confirm nearly instantaneously. You can navigate to your transaction history in the top right corner of the home screen to see the status of your transaction.
